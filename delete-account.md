# Delete Your Account

To delete your GST Invoice account and all associated data:

**Option 1 — Email us**
Send an email to Quelzar.support@gmail.com with subject:
"Delete My Account"

Include your registered Google email address.
We will delete your account and all data within 7 business days.

**Option 2 — In-app**
Open GST Invoice app → Settings → Logout
This removes your active session.
For complete data deletion, please email us as above.

**What gets deleted:**
- Your invoices
- Your clients
- Your business profile
- Your subscription status
